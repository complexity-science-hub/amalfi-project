Project Overview

Investigating graph learning methods for analyzing complex cryptoasset transaction networks across multiple blockchains and layers.

Key Objectives

  • Develop a comprehensive cryptoasset knowledge graph
  • Evaluate graph learning techniques for blockchain analytics
  • Introduce multi-layer conceptualization for cryptoasset transactions
  • Address challenges of limited ground-truth data in crypto-ecosystems
